Freeze Dry — Standalone Hydrator

Reconstruct art from Solana memos. SHA-256 verified. Zero dependencies. Works offline after fetch.

— or —

Uses browser-native AVIF decode + gzip decompress. No WASM, no CDN, no servers.
Public Solana RPC is slow (~5s/chunk). For faster reads, use a Helius or QuickNode RPC URL.
Supports v3 (FD: headers), v2, and v1 chunk formats.
